Home Audience Design

Target Audience

The target audience for the Campinas Chamber of Commerce website would primarily be local businesses and entrepreneurs in the Campinas region. These could include small and medium-sized businesses across a variety of industries, as well as individual entrepreneurs and start-up companies. Additionally, the website may also be of interest to individuals and organizations that support local businesses, such as investors, government agencies, and community leaders. Overall, the target audience would be individuals and businesses that are looking to connect, collaborate, and grow in the Campinas region.

Personas

Persona: "Maria", a small business owner

  • Maria is a 35-year-old business owner who runs a small clothing store in Campinas.
  • She is married with two children and is the primary breadwinner for her family.
  • Maria is highly motivated and passionate about her business, but struggles with keeping up with the latest trends in retail and marketing.
  • She is looking for ways to increase sales and attract more customers to her store.

Persona: "José", a young entrepreneur

  • José is a 25-year-old recent college graduate who has started a tech company in Campinas.
  • He is single and lives alone.
  • José is ambitious, driven, and excited to make a difference in the world with his business.
  • He is looking for support and resources to help him grow his business and take it to the next level.

Scenarios

  1. A business owner visits the Campinas Chamber of Commerce website to find out about local business opportunities and resources.
  2. An entrepreneur visits the website to find out about upcoming business networking events in the area.
  3. A business owner visits the website to find relevant articles and resources to improve their marketing strategies.
  4. An entrepreneur visits the website to learn more about the benefits and membership options of joining the Chamber of Commerce.
  5. A business owner visits the website to find a directory of local suppliers and partners.
